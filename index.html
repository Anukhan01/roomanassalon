About the Expert

Meet Roomana Ali —
The Woman Behind
the Transformation

With over 18 years of dedicated expertise, Roomana Ali has built a name synonymous with bridal elegance, professional beauty, and transformative wellness. Her journey began with a passion for artistry and a commitment to making every woman feel her most radiant self.

Trained by the world's leading beauty brands and academies — from L'Oréal Professionnel to Stage Line Spain-Barcelona to Janssen Advanced Skincare Aachen — Roomana brings international expertise right to the heart of Karachi.

As the founder of Roomana Beauty & Fitness Club in PECHS Block 2, she has created a sanctuary where beauty, wellness, and confidence come together under one roof.
